1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly and can remember your preferences.

2. Our Cookie Usage

PDF Tools uses an absolute minimum of cookies. Here's what we use:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
theme_preference Remembers your light/dark mode preference 1 year Functional
tool_settings Saves your last used tool settings Session Functional

3. What We Don't Use

We intentionally do NOT use:

  • Analytics cookies: No Google Analytics or similar tracking
  • Advertising cookies: No ad targeting or retargeting
  • Third-party cookies: No social media or external service cookies
  • Session tracking: We don't track your browsing behavior

4. Local Storage

We use browser Local Storage (not cookies) for:

  • Temporary file processing data (cleared when you close the tab)
  • User interface preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and security ‚Üí Cookies
  • Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Cookies
  • Edge: Settings ‚Üí Cookies and site permissions

Blocking cookies may affect some features, but core functionality will remain available.
