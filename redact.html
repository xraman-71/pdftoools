How to Redact

1
Upload your file
Choose the Redact PDF or Redact Image tab, then drag & drop your file onto the upload area — or tap/click it to browse your device.
2
Draw redaction boxes
Make sure the Redact tool is active (black, selected in the toolbar). Then draw a rectangle over any area you want to black out.
3
Adjust if needed
Too many boxes? Hit Clear All to start over, or tap the ✕ on any individual box to remove just that one. Use + / − to zoom in for precision.
4
Apply & Download
When ready, tap Apply Redactions. Your file is processed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded. Then download the redacted file.
Drawing on your device
PC / Mac — Click & drag iPhone / Android — Tap & drag finger iPad / Tablet — Tap & drag finger or stylus
💡 Tip: If the page scrolls instead of drawing, make sure the Redact button is selected in the toolbar, not Select.
